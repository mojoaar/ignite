01

How It Works

1. Interview

Ignite conducts a 5-phase AI conversation: Identity, Tech Stack, Features, Architecture, and Generation. Answer at your pace — all progress is persisted.

2. Generate

Pick your AI provider and model. Watch as four documentation files are produced from the conversation context — project spec, agent guide, plan, and README.

3. Ship

Files land in your project directory. Switch providers mid-conversation. Resume any project from history. Export chats as markdown. Ready to commit.
